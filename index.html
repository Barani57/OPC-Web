Our Story

They Cared for Everyone.
Now It's Our Turn.

Old People Charitable Trust was born from a simple belief — elders who spent their lives caring for families deserve to spend their final years in comfort, dignity, and love.

"It's not how much we give, but how much love we put into giving."

Based in Trichy, Tamil Nadu, we provide 23 elderly residents with three nutritious meals a day, a clean home, and the companionship they deserve. Every day, without exception.
